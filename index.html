(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Dead End
Dead End is a 3 to 4 person combat game. Based in the city of Seattle, one player takes on the role of the necromancer attempting to summon various undead creatures in order to take over the city. The remaining players must travel around the map to build up strength in order to defeat their nemesis.
